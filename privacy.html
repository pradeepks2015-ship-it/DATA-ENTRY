Privacy Statement

This is an internal utility tool operated for the field staff of M.P. Poorv Kshetra Vidyut Vitaran Co. Ltd. (MPEZ), Seoni Circle, Madhya Pradesh, India. It is not a public service and does not imitate any other website.

What this site is

This web application is used by our own employees to record day-to-day electricity distribution operations — such as substation feeder meter readings, material/store records, payment collection tracking, and field incident reports (e.g., broken poles).

What information is collected

The site stores only operational utility records entered by our own staff: meter readings, consumer service references (IVRS/account numbers), field photos with GPS location of electrical assets, and staff work notes. Data is saved on the staff member's own device and synchronized to our internal Google Sheets workspace.

What is NOT collected

The site does not collect financial information, does not process payments, does not ask visitors for credentials of any other website or service, and does not use advertising or tracking cookies. The simple password prompts on the site are role-based access gates for our own staff only.

Who can access the data

Recorded data is used only by authorized MPEZ staff of the concerned Distribution Centres for departmental work. It is not sold or shared with third parties.
